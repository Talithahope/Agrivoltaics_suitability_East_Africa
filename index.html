What is this map?

This interactive map shows the results of a multi-criteria spatial analysis assessing where agrivoltaic systems, the co-location of agriculture and solar panels, are biophysically suitable across East Africa according to two suitability scenarios.

Scenarios

Climate Vulnerable prioritises areas where farming communities face the greatest climate risk.

Maximum Productivity prioritises areas where agrivoltaic systems would generate the most combined energy and agricultural output.

Difference (CV − MP) highlights where the two scenarios diverge. Positive values (purple) mean an area scores higher under the climate vulnerable weighting; negative values (orange) mean the land scores higher under the maximum productivity weighting.

Suitability scores

Scores range from 4 (low suitability) to 9 (high suitability). Areas scoring 0 were classified as unsuitable.

Trial sites

The orange triangles mark real-world agrivoltaic trial sites in Kenya (Isinya) and Tanzania (Morogoro).
